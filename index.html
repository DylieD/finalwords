The Signature Lid

Their final words, in your handwriting.

Every casket we build carries our name for a reason. Before the service, family and friends are invited to write directly onto the lid, a short note, a memory, a goodbye, so the people who mattered most get the last word too. Each casket also carries a personalised "Final Words" nameplate, presented with a pen for signing.

  • Signable write-on lid on every casket, every range
  • Personalised nameplate with signing pen included
  • Solid timber construction, hand-finished joinery
  • Available across the Signature, Queen's and Kings ranges

About Final Words

A South African company, built on care.

Final Words was founded on a simple idea: the people left behind deserve a way to say goodbye that feels personal. We work directly with families and funeral homes across South Africa, building caskets that give everyone who loved them a chance to leave a final word of their own.
